Cold Email Best Practices

Subject line is everything. 47% of emails are opened based on subject line alone. Keep it under 50 characters and make it feel personal, not promotional.
Lead with them, not you. Start with something about their business, not your pitch. Prove you did your homework in the first sentence.
One clear CTA. Ask for one thing only — a 15-min call, a reply, a yes/no question. Multiple asks kill conversion.
Keep it short. Under 150 words is ideal. Prospects decide in 3 seconds. Long emails get scrolled past.
Follow up 3 times. 80% of deals close after the 5th touch. A polite follow-up sequence 2–4 days apart dramatically increases response rates.
