Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At QuickDraftAI ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit and use our website at quickdraftai.org.

Information We Collect

QuickDraftAI is designed to be used without requiring user accounts or personal information. However, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Content you submit: When you use our writing tools, the text you enter (job descriptions, email topics, etc.) is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the requested output. We do not store this content on our servers.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous information about how you interact with our site, such as pages visited, time spent, and tools used.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and approximate location for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our writing tools
  • Generate AI-powered content based on your inputs
  • Analyze site usage to enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

QuickDraftAI uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API: We send your input text to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data they receive. Visit anthropic.com/legal/privacy for details.
  • Google AdSense: We display ads served by Google. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.
  • GitHub Pages: Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs.
  • Netlify: We use Netlify Functions to securely process API requests.

Cookies

QuickDraftAI itself does not use cookies. However, Google AdSense and other third-party services may set cookies on your device. These cookies help personalize ads and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We do not store the content you submit through our writing tools. All input is processed in real-time and discarded after the response is generated.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

Since we don't store user data, most of these rights are automatically respected by our service design.

Children's Privacy

QuickDraftAI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our website.
