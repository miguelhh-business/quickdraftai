Our Tools

Email Subject Line Generator

Generates 7 high-converting email subject lines using proven psychological triggers like curiosity, urgency, social proof, and benefit-focused language. Built for marketers, salespeople, and newsletter writers who need to maximize open rates.

Cover Letter Generator

Creates ATS-friendly cover letters between 280-380 words, tailored to any specific job description. The tool naturally incorporates keywords from the job posting to help your application pass through applicant tracking systems while still sounding human and personal.

LinkedIn Bio Generator

Produces three different versions of your LinkedIn About section: a short one-liner for quick previews, a medium professional version, and a longer narrative version with personal storytelling. Perfect for job seekers, freelancers, and professionals building their personal brand.

Cold Email Writer

Generates three personalized cold email variations for sales outreach, business development, and networking. Each variation uses a different angle to maximize response rates, with subject lines included.
